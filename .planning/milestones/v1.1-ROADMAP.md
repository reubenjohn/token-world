## Overview

Token World v1.1 took the mandate *"make it feel possible — maybe even inevitable —
that interesting universes can emerge from play without hand-holding"* and delivered
three surfaces: the Operator CLI (agents query a running universe), the NiceGUI
Dashboard (humans watch it move), and the Emergence Substrate (subagents close the
author-resume loop over the user's Claude subscription at $0 marginal cost).

The milestone ran in "hybrid mode" per the user's session-4 choice (option `c`):
direct-edit + subagent dispatch + retroactive phase scaffolding rather than
`/gsd-new-milestone` front-loading. Phases 08–12 are scaffolded here retroactively;
plans landed as commits with their own retrospective PLAN.md stubs where needed.
An unattended Willowbrook overnight run authored 11 novel mechanics end-to-end
(examine, pet, sharpen, walk, draw, plant, force, drop, water, hum, lift) — the
substrate worked.

Four v1.0 Known Gaps also closed out during the warm-up phase: 04.1 SC-2 smoke test
via claude-cli backend, traceability + roadmap-progress CI scripts, research-doc
refresh, and BatchSummary.agent_id stub.
