## Patterns Noticed

Edge-cases are **overwhelmingly engine-layer** — 11 of 17 dedup'd gaps sit in the engine, and all but one are address-now. The dominant theme is **grounding under failure**: every UC asks "what happens when the default path is not available?" (missing target, concurrent actors, hidden state, nonsense input, reactive cycle, locked door) and the answer each time implicates the same three engine surfaces — **classification verdicts** (`no_such_target`, `no_viable_action`, conflict resolution), **observation projection** (visible_to, chain_truncated trace, grounding templates), and **mechanic refusal/blocking narratives**. Consolidating those three surfaces would close more than half this category's gaps and also retire R-E02 (graceful refusal), V-E05 (illumination filter), and O-E06 (visibility) from the other four categories. The edge-case UCs are therefore the **integration tests** for the Phase 4 engine — if the engine's classifier/observation/refusal pipeline is built with these scenarios in mind from day one, the other four categories inherit the grounding almost for free. The `defer` items are narrow enough that each can be tackled independently once v1 lands.
